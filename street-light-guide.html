Street Light Complaint Guide

Learn how to report faulty or damaged street lights, upload supporting evidence, track your complaint, and receive updates through your Municipal Corporation, Electricity Department, or local government portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation's official grievance portal or the Electricity Department's complaint portal. Most local authorities allow citizens to report street light issues online and track their complaint status.

Tip: Report faulty street lights as soon as possible to improve road safety and public security.

📝 Step 2 — Register Your Complaint

Select the Street Light Complaint service and carefully complete the complaint form.

  • Enter your contact details (if required).
  • Provide the exact location of the faulty street light.
  • Select the complaint category.
  • Describe the issue clearly.
  • Review the complaint before submitting.

📷 Step 3 — Upload Supporting Evidence

Attach photographs or additional information to help the concerned authority identify the problem quickly.

  • Photos of the damaged or non-functional street light.
  • Nearest landmark or complete address.
  • Pole number (if available).
  • Additional comments (if required).
Providing the pole number or a nearby landmark can help authorities locate the street light more quickly.

💡 Step 4 — Common Street Light Issues

You can report various street lighting problems, including:

  • Street light not working.
  • Street light flickering.
  • Damaged or broken light pole.
  • Light remaining ON during daytime.
  • Exposed electrical wires (if applicable).
  • Other street lighting issues.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Complaint

After submitting your complaint, use the Complaint Number or Reference Number to monitor its progress.

  • Track complaint status.
  • View inspection updates.
  • Receive notifications from the authority.
  • Check for any requests for additional information.
Keep your Complaint Number safe until the issue has been resolved.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Provide the exact location of the faulty street light.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Include the pole number if visible.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate complaints.
  • Track your complaint regularly.
  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation or Electricity Department portal.
Always submit genuine complaints with accurate information. Never report false issues or misuse the complaint system.
