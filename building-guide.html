Building Plan Approval Guide

Learn how to apply for Building Plan Approval, upload required documents, track your application, pay applicable fees, and download approval certificates through your Municipal Corporation or local development authority portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation or Development Authority's official Building Plan Approval portal. The portal provides services for building plan submissions, approvals, application tracking, fee payments, and certificate downloads.

Tip: Building Plan Approval is generally required before starting new construction, major renovations, or extensions to an existing building, subject to local building regulations.

📝 Step 2 — Submit Your Building Plan Application

Select the Building Plan Approval service and carefully complete the online application.

  • Enter applicant details.
  • Provide property information.
  • Enter plot and building details.
  • Select the type of construction.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Prepare scanned copies of the required documents before applying.

  • Identity Proof.
  • Address Proof.
  • Property Ownership Documents.
  • Site Plan.
  • Building Plan prepared by an authorized professional (if required).
  • Other documents required by your local authority.
Ensure that all drawings and documents are complete, accurate, and clearly readable to avoid delays during verification.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Applicable Fees

Complete the online payment for scrutiny charges, approval fees, or other applicable charges.

  • Pay the required fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use your Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor your application's progress.

  • Track verification status.
  • Check inspection updates (if applicable).
  • Monitor approval progress.
  • Respond to additional document requests if required.
Some applications may require site inspections or additional approvals before final approval is granted.

📥 Step 6 — Download the Approval Certificate

Once your application is approved, download your Building Plan Approval certificate or approval letter from the portal.

  • Verify all approved details.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print copies for future reference if required.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply before starting any construction work.
  • Verify all property and applicant details before submission.
  • Keep your application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Respond promptly if additional documents are requested.
  • Download and securely store the approval certificate.
  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation or Development Authority portal.
Always submit building plan applications through the official government portal. Never share your OTP, payment details, or login credentials with unauthorized websites or individuals.
