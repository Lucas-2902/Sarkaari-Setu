mAadhaar User Guide

Learn how to use the official mAadhaar app to carry your Aadhaar digitally, update your profile, generate secure QR codes, and access Aadhaar-related services on your smartphone.

📱 Step 1 — Install the Official mAadhaar App

Download the official mAadhaar app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Ensure that the developer is the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI).

Tip: Always install the official app to keep your Aadhaar information secure.

👤 Step 2 — Add Your Aadhaar Profile

After opening the app, add your Aadhaar profile by completing the verification process.

  • Enter your Aadhaar Number or Virtual ID (VID).
  • Verify your registered mobile number using OTP.
  • Create a secure 4-digit or 6-digit PIN for quick access.
  • Your digital Aadhaar profile will be added to the app.

🪪 Step 3 — Access Your Digital Aadhaar

Once your profile is added, you can access your Aadhaar details directly from the app.

  • View your Aadhaar profile.
  • Show your digital Aadhaar when required.
  • Access your demographic details.
  • Carry Aadhaar without keeping a physical copy.
Your digital Aadhaar can be useful whenever Aadhaar identification is accepted.

📷 Step 4 — Generate & Share Secure QR Code

The app allows you to generate a secure QR code that can be shared for Aadhaar verification where accepted.

  • Generate Secure QR Code.
  • Share QR Code securely.
  • Allow quick Aadhaar verification.

🔧 Step 5 — Use Aadhaar Services

The app provides access to several Aadhaar-related services.

  • Retrieve Aadhaar if forgotten.
  • Download e-Aadhaar.
  • Locate Aadhaar Enrolment Centres.
  • Check Aadhaar Status.
  • Verify Aadhaar.
  • Generate or Retrieve Virtual ID (VID).

🔒 Step 6 — Keep Your Aadhaar Secure

Protect your Aadhaar information by following these safety practices.

  • Never share your OTP.
  • Do not disclose your app PIN.
  • Lock your smartphone with a secure password.
  • Use only the official mAadhaar app.
If you change your registered mobile number, update it through the official UIDAI process.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Keep your registered mobile number active for OTP verification.
  • Update the app regularly for new features and security improvements.
  • Use Virtual ID (VID) whenever supported for added privacy.
  • Verify your Aadhaar details before sharing them.
  • Use only official UIDAI services for Aadhaar-related requests.
Never share your Aadhaar OTP, app PIN, or personal information with anyone claiming to offer Aadhaar services. Use only official UIDAI platforms.
