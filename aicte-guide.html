AICTE Website User Guide

Learn how to use the official All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) website for college approvals, scholarships, internships, student schemes, and technical education services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official AICTE Website

Open the official AICTE website. The homepage provides the latest notices, approvals, circulars, student schemes, internship opportunities, and technical education updates.

Tip: Check the homepage regularly for new scholarship schemes, internship announcements, and official notifications.

🏫 Step 2 — Verify AICTE Approved Colleges

Before taking admission into a technical institution, verify whether the college is approved by AICTE.

  • Search for your college.
  • Verify AICTE approval status.
  • Check courses approved by AICTE.
  • View institution details and approval validity.

🎓 Step 3 — Explore Student Schemes

AICTE offers several schemes and initiatives for students pursuing technical education.

  • Scholarship Information
  • Pragati Scholarship
  • Saksham Scholarship
  • Student Development Programs
  • Innovation & Startup Initiatives

💼 Step 4 — Find Internship Opportunities

Students can access internship-related resources through AICTE initiatives.

  • Internship Opportunities
  • Industry Training Programs
  • Skill Development Programs
  • Career Enhancement Resources
Regularly check internship opportunities as new listings are added throughout the year.

📢 Step 5 — Read Official Notifications

Stay updated with important announcements regarding:

  • College Approvals
  • Academic Policies
  • Faculty Development Programs
  • Scholarships
  • Student Competitions
  • Technical Education Initiatives

📥 Step 6 — Download Important Documents

Keep copies of important AICTE documents whenever required.

  • Approval Handbooks
  • Guidelines
  • Official Circulars
  • Scheme Notifications
  • Policy Documents

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Always verify that your college is AICTE-approved before admission.
  • Apply for scholarships before the deadline.
  • Use official AICTE internship portals for genuine opportunities.
  • Read eligibility criteria carefully before applying for any scheme.
  • Keep checking the website for new student initiatives and announcements.
  • Download important notices for future reference.
Always use the official AICTE website to verify college approvals, scholarships, and student schemes. Avoid relying on unofficial websites for approval status or admission-related information.
