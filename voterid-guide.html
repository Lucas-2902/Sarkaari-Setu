Voter ID Services User Guide

Learn how to use the official Voter Services Portal to apply for a new Voter ID, update your details, download your e-EPIC, search your name in the electoral roll, and access other election-related services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Voter Services Portal

Open the official Voter Services Portal. The homepage provides access to voter registration, voter ID corrections, electoral roll search, e-EPIC download, and other election services.

Tip: Keep your registered mobile number active, as OTP verification may be required for many services.

👤 Step 2 — Register or Log In

Create an account or sign in to access online voter services.

  • Create a new account.
  • Verify your mobile number using OTP.
  • Log in to access available services.

🗳️ Step 3 — Apply for a New Voter ID

If you are eligible to vote and are not yet registered, complete the online voter registration application.

  • Enter your personal details.
  • Provide your residential address.
  • Upload the required documents.
  • Review the application before submission.
Ensure all details match your official documents to avoid delays in processing.

✏️ Step 4 — Update or Correct Your Details

If your information has changed, you can request updates through the portal.

  • Correct your name or personal details.
  • Update your address.
  • Change your photograph (where applicable).
  • Request replacement of a lost or damaged Voter ID.

📥 Step 5 — Download Your e-EPIC

Eligible voters can download their digital Voter ID (e-EPIC) from the portal.

  • Log in to your account.
  • Verify your identity.
  • Download the digital Voter ID.
  • Save a copy for future use.

🔍 Step 6 — Search the Electoral Roll & Track Applications

Use the portal to verify your voter registration status and track submitted applications.

  • Search your name in the Electoral Roll.
  • Verify polling station details.
  • Track application status.
  • View updates on submitted requests.
Keep your Reference Number safe so you can track your application easily.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply for voter registration well before election dates.
  • Verify your details before submitting any application.
  • Keep your mobile number updated for OTP verification.
  • Download your e-EPIC after it becomes available.
  • Check your polling station before election day.
  • Use only official Election Commission portals for voter services.
Never share your OTP or login credentials with anyone. Always use the official Voter Services Portal or Election Commission of India website for voter-related services.
