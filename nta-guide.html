National Testing Agency (NTA) Guide

Learn how to use the National Testing Agency (NTA) portal to register for examinations, download admit cards, check answer keys, view results, and access other important examination services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official NTA Website

Open the official National Testing Agency (NTA) website. The portal provides information about entrance examinations, registration, admit cards, answer keys, results, notices, and public announcements.

Tip: Always use the official NTA website for registrations, fee payments, and downloading examination documents.

📝 Step 2 — Register for an Examination

When registrations are open, select your examination and complete the online application form.

  • Create a new account (if required).
  • Fill in your personal details.
  • Enter educational qualifications.
  • Select your examination city preferences.
  • Review the application carefully before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Documents

Upload the required documents in the prescribed format mentioned in the examination information bulletin.

  • Recent passport-size photograph.
  • Signature.
  • Category or reservation certificate (if applicable).
  • PwD certificate (if applicable).
  • Other documents required for the examination.
Ensure that all uploaded files meet the specified size, format, and quality requirements.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Examination Fee

Complete the online payment using one of the available payment methods.

  • Net Banking.
  • Debit Card.
  • Credit Card.
  • UPI.
Download and save the payment receipt after a successful transaction.

🎫 Step 5 — Download the Admit Card

When released, log in using your application credentials to download your admit card.

  • Verify all personal details.
  • Check the exam date and reporting time.
  • Confirm the examination centre.
  • Print multiple copies for safety.

📊 Step 6 — Answer Key & Results

After the examination, use the portal to access important post-exam services.

  • View the provisional answer key.
  • Submit challenges (if the facility is available).
  • Download your scorecard.
  • Check examination results.
  • Download official notices and public announcements.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Read the Information Bulletin before applying.
  • Complete your application before the deadline.
  • Keep your Application Number and Password secure.
  • Regularly check the official website for updates.
  • Download important documents as soon as they are available.
  • Use only the official NTA website.
Never share your Application Number, Password, OTP, or payment details with anyone. Beware of unofficial websites claiming to provide examination services.
