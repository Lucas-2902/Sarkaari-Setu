DigiLocker User Guide

Learn how to use DigiLocker to securely store, access, and share your official government-issued documents online.

🌐 Step 1 — Sign In or Create an Account

Visit the official DigiLocker website or app and sign in using your mobile number or Aadhaar. New users can create an account by verifying their mobile number with an OTP.

Tip: Use your own mobile number as important notifications and OTPs are sent to it.

🪪 Step 2 — Complete Your Profile

After logging in, complete your profile by entering your personal details and linking your Aadhaar (optional but recommended for additional services).

  • Verify your mobile number.
  • Add your personal details.
  • Link Aadhaar (if desired).
  • Create a secure username and PIN.

📄 Step 3 — Get Official Documents

Use the Issued Documents section to fetch digital documents directly from government departments.

  • Aadhaar
  • Driving Licence
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC)
  • PAN Verification
  • CBSE/NIOS Marksheets & Certificates
  • Insurance Documents
Documents issued directly through DigiLocker are digitally verified and accepted by many government departments.

📤 Step 4 — Upload Your Own Documents

You can also upload personal documents that are not automatically available through DigiLocker.

  • PDF Files
  • Scanned Certificates
  • Passport Copy
  • Educational Documents
  • Identity Documents

🔗 Step 5 — Share Documents Securely

Instead of printing documents, you can securely share them online whenever requested.

  • Generate a secure document link.
  • Share verified documents with organizations.
  • Avoid sending unnecessary paper copies.

🔒 Step 6 — Keep Your Account Secure

Protect your DigiLocker account by following these safety practices.

  • Never share your OTP.
  • Keep your login credentials private.
  • Log out from public devices.
  • Regularly update your mobile number if it changes.
Your DigiLocker account may contain important personal documents, so always keep it secure.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Download important documents before travelling if internet access may be limited.
  • Use Issued Documents whenever available instead of uploading scanned copies.
  • Check that your personal details match government records.
  • Keep your mobile number active to receive OTPs.
  • Use DigiLocker for admissions, government services, and document verification whenever accepted.
Always access DigiLocker through the official website or mobile application. Never share your OTP, password, or login details with anyone.
