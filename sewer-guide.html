Sewer Connection & Sewer Service User Guide

Learn how to apply for a new sewer connection, request sewer-related services, track your application, and register complaints through your local Water Board or Municipal Corporation portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Water Board or Municipal Corporation's official website. The portal provides online services for sewer connections, complaint registration, application tracking, and service requests.

Tip: Different cities and states have different authorities (such as Jal Boards, Water Supply Boards, or Municipal Corporations) responsible for sewer services.

📝 Step 2 — Apply for a New Sewer Connection

Select the New Sewer Connection service and complete the online application form.

  • Enter property details.
  • Provide owner information.
  • Enter property address.
  • Fill in the required connection details.
  • Review the application before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Keep scanned copies of the required documents ready before applying.

  • Identity Proof.
  • Address Proof.
  • Property Ownership Documents.
  • Building Approval Documents (if applicable).
  • Recent Water Bill (if required).
  • Other documents requested by the local authority.
Ensure that all uploaded documents are clear and valid to avoid delays in verification.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Required Charges

If applicable, complete the online payment for connection or service charges.

  • Pay connection fees.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application or Complaint

Use the application or complaint number to monitor the status of your request.

  • Track connection approval.
  • Check inspection status.
  • Monitor complaint resolution.
  • View service updates.

🚨 Step 6 — Register Sewer Complaints

Most official portals allow citizens to report sewer-related issues online.

  • Sewer Blockage.
  • Sewer Overflow.
  • Damaged Sewer Line.
  • Manhole Issues.
  • Unpleasant Odour Complaints.
  • Other Sewer-related Problems.
Keep your complaint reference number safe to track the progress of your complaint.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply only through the official government portal.
  • Verify your property details before submission.
  • Keep copies of uploaded documents and payment receipts.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Report sewer problems as soon as possible.
  • Keep your application or complaint number safe.
Always use your local Water Board or Municipal Corporation's official website for sewer-related services. Never make payments through unofficial websites or share your OTP with anyone.
