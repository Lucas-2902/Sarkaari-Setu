Jeevan Pramaan User Guide

Learn how to use the official Jeevan Pramaan portal to generate, submit, download, and manage your Digital Life Certificate (DLC) for pension services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Jeevan Pramaan Portal

Open the official Jeevan Pramaan website. The portal provides services for generating Digital Life Certificates, locating enrollment centers, downloading certificates, and checking submission status.

Tip: Keep your Aadhaar number and registered mobile number ready before starting the process.

👤 Step 2 — Verify Your Identity

To generate a Digital Life Certificate, your identity must be verified using Aadhaar authentication through a supported biometric device.

  • Enter your Aadhaar Number.
  • Provide your Pension Payment Order (PPO) Number.
  • Select your Pension Sanctioning Authority.
  • Complete biometric authentication.

📄 Step 3 — Generate Your Digital Life Certificate

After successful verification, your Digital Life Certificate (DLC) is generated electronically.

  • Review your personal details.
  • Confirm pension information.
  • Generate your certificate.
  • Receive the Pramaan ID.
Save your Pramaan ID as it helps you retrieve and track your certificate later.

📥 Step 4 — Download Your Certificate

Once generated, you can download your Digital Life Certificate for your records.

  • Download the certificate PDF.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print a copy if required.

📍 Step 5 — Locate Jeevan Pramaan Centers

If you cannot complete the process at home, use the portal to find nearby Jeevan Pramaan Centers where biometric authentication services are available.

  • Search nearby centers.
  • Visit with required documents.
  • Complete biometric verification.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Keep your Aadhaar-linked mobile number active.
  • Ensure your biometric authentication device is functioning properly if generating the certificate from home.
  • Save your Pramaan ID for future reference.
  • Download a copy of your certificate after successful generation.
  • Submit your Digital Life Certificate before the deadline specified by your pension authority.
  • Use only the official Jeevan Pramaan portal or authorized service centers.
Never share your Aadhaar OTP, biometric data, or personal information with unauthorized individuals. Always use the official Jeevan Pramaan portal or authorized centers for Digital Life Certificate services.
