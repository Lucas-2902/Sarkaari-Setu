Name Correction in Birth Certificate Guide

Learn how to apply for a name correction in a Birth Certificate, upload the required documents, pay applicable fees, track your application, and download the updated certificate through your State Government or Municipal Corporation's official portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Government's Civil Registration System (CRS) portal or your Municipal Corporation's Birth & Death Registration portal. Most authorities provide online services for birth certificate corrections and certificate downloads.

Tip: Name corrections are generally allowed only after verification of supporting documents and may require approval from the competent authority.

📝 Step 2 — Submit the Correction Application

Select the Name Correction in Birth Certificate service and carefully complete the online application form.

  • Enter the Birth Registration Number or Certificate Number.
  • Provide the applicant's details.
  • Enter the existing name and the corrected name.
  • Specify the reason for the correction.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Prepare scanned copies of the required documents before applying.

  • Existing Birth Certificate.
  • Identity Proof of the applicant or parent/guardian (where applicable).
  • Address Proof.
  • Documents supporting the requested name correction.
  • Affidavit or declaration (if required by the authority).
  • Other documents requested by the Registrar.
Document requirements may vary depending on your state or local authority. Ensure all uploaded documents are clear and valid.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Applicable Fee

If a correction fee is applicable, complete the online payment using the available payment methods.

  • Pay the prescribed correction fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference number for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use your Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor the progress of your correction request.

  • Track verification status.
  • Respond to requests for additional documents.
  • Monitor approval updates.
  • Check application status online.
Keep your Application Number safe until the correction process has been completed.

📥 Step 6 — Download the Corrected Birth Certificate

Once your application is approved, download the updated Birth Certificate from the official portal.

  • Verify that the corrected name appears correctly.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print copies if required for future use.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Verify all information before submitting your application.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep your application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Download the corrected certificate after approval.
  • Use only the official Civil Registration System (CRS) or Municipal Corporation portal.
Always apply through the official government portal. Never share your OTP, login credentials, or payment details with unauthorized websites or individuals.
