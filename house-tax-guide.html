House Tax Correction Application Guide

Learn how to request corrections to your house/property tax records, upload supporting documents, track your application, and download the updated property tax record through your Municipal Corporation or local government portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation or local government's official Property Tax portal. The portal provides services for property tax payments, record corrections, ownership updates, and application tracking.

Tip: Property tax corrections can include changes to the owner's name, property details, address, assessment information, or other records, depending on your local authority.

📝 Step 2 — Submit a House Tax Correction Request

Select the House Tax Correction or Property Tax Record Correction service and complete the online application form.

  • Enter the Property ID or Assessment Number.
  • Provide the applicant's details.
  • Select the type of correction required.
  • Describe the correction clearly.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Supporting Documents

Keep scanned copies of the necessary documents ready before submitting your application.

  • Identity Proof.
  • Address Proof.
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt.
  • Property Ownership Documents.
  • Documents supporting the requested correction.
  • Other documents required by your local authority.
Ensure that all uploaded documents clearly support the correction you are requesting.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Applicable Fee (If Required)

Some municipal authorities may charge a processing fee for correction requests.

  • Complete the online payment if applicable.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference number.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use your Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor the progress of your correction request.

  • Track verification status.
  • Respond to requests for additional documents.
  • Monitor approval progress.
  • View application updates online.
Keep your application number safe until the correction has been completed.

📥 Step 6 — Download the Updated Property Tax Record

Once your request is approved, download or view the updated property tax record from the portal.

  • Verify the corrected information.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print a copy if required.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Verify all property details before submitting your request.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep your application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Download the updated record after approval.
  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation or Property Tax portal.
Always submit correction requests through your official Municipal Corporation or Property Tax portal. Never share your OTP, payment details, or login credentials with unauthorized websites or individuals.
