Park Maintenance Complaint Guide

Learn how to report issues related to public parks and gardens, upload supporting evidence, track your complaint, and receive updates through your Municipal Corporation or local government's official grievance portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation's official grievance or citizen services portal. Most local authorities provide online services for reporting park maintenance issues and tracking complaint status.

Tip: Report maintenance issues promptly to help keep public parks clean, safe, and enjoyable for everyone.

📝 Step 2 — Register Your Complaint

Select the Park Maintenance Complaint service and carefully complete the complaint form.

  • Enter your contact details (if required).
  • Provide the exact park name and location.
  • Select the complaint category.
  • Describe the issue clearly.
  • Review all information before submitting.

📷 Step 3 — Upload Supporting Evidence

Attach photographs and location details to help the concerned department identify and resolve the issue more efficiently.

  • Photos of the maintenance issue.
  • Park name and complete address.
  • Nearest landmark (if applicable).
  • Additional comments or supporting information.
Clear photographs and an accurate location help municipal staff locate the problem more quickly.

🌳 Step 4 — Common Park Maintenance Issues

You can report a variety of park-related problems, including:

  • Broken benches or playground equipment.
  • Damaged walking paths.
  • Overgrown grass or bushes.
  • Uncollected garbage or litter.
  • Damaged fences or gates.
  • Faulty park lighting.
  • Water leakage or drainage issues.
  • Other park maintenance concerns.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Complaint

After submitting your complaint, use the Complaint Number or Reference Number to monitor its progress.

  • Track complaint status.
  • View inspection updates.
  • Receive notifications from the authority.
  • Check for requests for additional information.
Keep your Complaint Number safe until the issue has been resolved.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Provide the correct park name and location.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Describe the problem in detail.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate complaints.
  • Track your complaint regularly.
  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation portal.
Submit only genuine complaints with accurate information. False or misleading complaints may delay maintenance services and affect other citizens.
