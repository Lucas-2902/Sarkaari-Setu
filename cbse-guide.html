CBSE Official Website Guide

Learn what services are available on the official CBSE website.

📢 Latest Circulars

The homepage displays the newest notifications, circulars, examination updates, public notices, and announcements released by CBSE.

📝 Examination Section

Students can find information related to board examinations including:

  • Class 10 & Class 12 Date Sheet
  • Admit Card Updates
  • Exam Guidelines
  • Practical Examination Notices
  • Important Instructions

🎓 Results

During result season, students can access:

  • Class 10 Results
  • Class 12 Results
  • Result Verification Information
  • Re-evaluation Notices
  • DigiLocker Information

📚 Academics

The Academic section provides learning resources for students and teachers.

  • Latest Syllabus
  • Sample Question Papers
  • Curriculum
  • Academic Circulars
  • Teacher Resources

🏫 School Services

  • Affiliation Information
  • School Directory
  • Important Circulars for Schools
  • Examination Related Services

📄 Student Services

  • Migration Certificate Information
  • Duplicate Documents
  • Verification Services
  • Frequently Asked Questions

🔍 How to Use the CBSE Website

  1. Open the official CBSE website.
  2. Select the section you need (Results, Academics, Examinations, etc.).
  3. Click the required notification or service.
  4. Read the instructions carefully.
  5. Follow the official process provided by CBSE.
