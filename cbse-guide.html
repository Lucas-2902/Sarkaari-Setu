How to Use the CBSE Website

Step 1: Open the Official Website

Visit the official CBSE website from your browser. The homepage displays the latest circulars, notices, examination updates, and important announcements.

Step 2: Choose the Service You Need

Use the navigation menu to select the category you're looking for, such as Results, Examinations, Academics, School Services, or Student Services.

Step 3: Read the Latest Notifications

Before proceeding, check the latest notices on the homepage. Important information such as examination schedules, admit card announcements, and result notifications are published here.

Step 4: Access Your Required Service

Click the relevant section and follow the instructions provided. Some services may require information such as your Roll Number, School Number, Admit Card ID, or Registration Number.

Step 5: Download or Print Documents

If your required document is available, download the PDF or print a copy for future reference. Always verify that the document is from the official CBSE website.

Need Help?

If you're unsure where to find a particular service, use the search option on the website or ask our AI assistant for guidance.

Note: This guide is intended to help users navigate the official CBSE website. It does not replace the instructions or notifications published by CBSE.
