Marriage Certificate Application Guide

Learn how to apply for a Marriage Certificate, upload required documents, schedule appointments (where applicable), track your application, and download your certificate through the official government portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Government's e-District Portal or Municipal Corporation website. These portals provide online services for marriage registration, certificate applications, appointment booking, and application tracking.

Tip: Marriage registration procedures and eligibility requirements may vary by state. Always refer to your State Government's official portal.

📝 Step 2 — Complete the Marriage Registration Form

Select the Marriage Registration service and carefully fill in the online application form.

  • Enter details of both spouses.
  • Provide the date and place of marriage.
  • Fill in permanent and current addresses.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Keep scanned copies of the required documents ready before submitting your application.

  • Identity Proof of both spouses.
  • Address Proof.
  • Age Proof.
  • Passport-size Photographs.
  • Marriage Photograph (if required).
  • Marriage Invitation Card (where applicable).
  • Witness Identity Proof (if required).
  • Other documents specified by your State Government.
Ensure all uploaded documents are clear and readable to avoid delays during verification.

📅 Step 4 — Book an Appointment (If Required)

Some states require applicants to schedule an appointment for document verification or certificate issuance.

  • Select an available date.
  • Choose a convenient time slot.
  • Download or save the appointment confirmation.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

After submitting your application, use the application or acknowledgement number to monitor its progress.

  • Check verification status.
  • Respond to any document requests.
  • Monitor approval updates.

📥 Step 6 — Download Your Marriage Certificate

Once approved, download the digitally issued Marriage Certificate from the portal.

  • Verify all details on the certificate.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print copies if required for official use.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply through your State Government's official portal only.
  • Verify all personal details before submitting the application.
  • Keep your application number safe for tracking.
  • Carry original documents if an in-person verification is required.
  • Download and securely store your certificate after approval.
  • Check your state's document requirements before applying.
Always use the official State Government or Municipal Corporation portal for marriage registration. Never share your OTP, login credentials, or payment information with unauthorized websites or individuals.
