NCERT Class 1 to 12 Textbooks Guide

Access all official, free digital textbooks directly from the National Council of Educational Research and Training.

Step 1 — Access the Portal

Click the portal link below to open the official NCERT e-books page.

Step 2 — Select Class

Select your target Class (Class I to XII) from the first dropdown menu.

Step 3 — Pick Subject & Title

Choose your desired Subject and your specific Book Title from the next fields.

Step 4 — Load Layout

Click the "Go" button to load the full book preview and chapter selection layout.

Step 5 — Execute Download

Click "Download Complete Book" at the bottom of the left sidebar menu to save the full textbook as a single zip/PDF archive.
