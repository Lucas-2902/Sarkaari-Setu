📊 CBSE Board Exam Results Guide

How to access and download your official Class 10th or Class 12th board examination marksheets.

📋 Verification Requirements

Have your examination credentials ready as printed on your official admit card:

  • Roll Number: Your unique 8-digit board roll number.
  • School Number: 5-digit official affiliation number of your school.
  • Admit Card ID: The alphanumeric security ID printed on your hall ticket.
  • Center Number: 4-digit exam center code where you appeared for the test.

Step 1 — Access the Dedicated Server

Click the results link below to open the dedicated CBSE Results portal hosting active server links.

Go to CBSE Results Portal →

Step 2 — Select Class Level

Click on the active link labeled either "Class X Result" or "Class XII Result" for the relevant examination cycle.

Step 3 — Input Tracking Credentials

Type your Roll Number, School Number, Center Number, and Admit Card ID into their matching text fields.

Step 4 — Submit and Download

Click the "Submit" button to load your digital marksheet. Review your subject-wise grades, then use your browser's print option to save it as a PDF or secure a physical printout.

📚 Current Official Syllabus & Curriculum Guide

How to download the exact unit-wise marks distribution and the officially deleted topics list.

📋 Download Planning

Ensure your device can handle standard text documents and know your specific academic track:

  • Secondary Track: Curriculum files for Class 9 and Class 10 subjects.
  • Senior Secondary Track: Curriculum files for Class 11 and Class 12 subjects.
  • PDF Viewer: A browser extension or app capable of opening PDF formatting.

Step 1 — Open the Academic Domain

Click the academic link below to open the specialized CBSE Academic framework dashboard.

Go to CBSE Academic Portal →

Step 2 — Locate Curriculum Menu

Hover over or click on the "Curriculum" tab located in the main top navigation menu bar.

Step 3 — Choose Class Bracket

Select the current academic year link, then choose either "Secondary Curriculum (IX-X)" or "Senior Secondary Curriculum (XI-XII)".

Step 4 — Select Subject Area

Navigate through the subject groups (Languages, Academic Electives, or Main Subjects) and click your target subject name.

Step 5 — Save Document

The unit-wise marks distribution file will load inside a new tab. Click the download icon to save it directly to your local storage directory.

📝 Sample Question Papers (SQPs) Guide

Access mock board blueprints along with official step-by-step marking answers.

📋 Blueprint Requirements

Be aware of your current registration configuration to match target templates:

  • Target Class: Confirm your examination bracket (Class X or Class XII).
  • Subject Scheme: Know your specific subject combinations and electives.

Step 1 — Navigate to Sample Archive

Use the link below to enter the verified database of active board practice material.

Go to CBSE Sample Papers Portal →

Step 2 — Select Academic Term

Locate your target academic examination year from the drop-down listings or grid tabs.

Step 3 — Filter Class Level

Click on either the "Class X" or "Class XII" text buttons to populate the matching subject table layout.

Step 4 — Access Question Blueprints

Locate your subject row. Click the link labeled "SQP" under the sample question paper column to review the complete structure of the test paper.

Step 5 — Download Marking Solutions

Return to the table row and click the link labeled "MS" under the adjacent column. This downloads the step-by-step answer key to learn the exact distribution of points for written answers.

🛠️ Duplicate Documents & Correction Portal (DADS)

Complete processing steps to replace lost scorecards, secure migration files, or fix name spellings online.

📋 Verification Proofs & Application Setup

Prepare digital scans of verification proofs before logging an administrative challenge:

  • Passing Metrics: Exact roll number, school code, and year of graduation.
  • Affidavit / Gazette: Legal name correction order copy if updating records.
  • Digital Scans: Clear file images of your current flawed certificate and signature block.

Step 1 — Access the DADS Infrastructure

Click the administrative button below to enter the official Duplicate Academic Document System (DADS) digital network.

Go to CBSE DADS Portal →

Step 2 — Isolate Your Request Type

Choose either "Digital Document" (for immediate online access) or "Printed Document" (to request a hardcopy dispatch via Speed Post).

Step 3 — Input Registration Baseline

Provide your original passing year, roll number, school code, and registration details to fetch your system files.

Step 4 — Detail Modality & Upload

Fill out your current shipping address data. Upload your scanned documentation proofs or legal correction records if changing your name spelling.

Step 5 — Processing Fee Remittance

Review the calculated processing charges, pay securely online via the integrated gateway, and track your tracking receipt ID for verification.

✏️ Private Candidate Exam Registration Guide

Instructions to register independently for improvements, supplementary, or private board exams.

📋 Independent Eligibility Requirements

Private status applies exclusively to candidates matching the criteria below:

  • Previous Year Records: Fallouts, compartment clearances, or improvement seekers from previous years.
  • Identification Details: Valid contact points and an address located within the testing boundaries.
  • Image Dimensions: Scanned passport photograph and clean signature image matching standard dimensional limits.

Step 1 — Access the Private Candidate Interface

Click the portal link below to land on the official CBSE application grid for private candidate entries.

Go to CBSE Private Candidate Portal →

Step 2 — Categorize Application Type

Select your category from the left dashboard sidebar: Compartment, Improvement, Failure, or Additional Subject.

Step 3 — Import Historical Records

Type in your previous main exam roll number, 5-digit school code, and center code to load your academic baseline records.

Step 4 — Configure Subjects & Center Preference

Select the exact subject codes you wish to appear for, and select your preferred geographic testing city and state area.

Step 5 — Identity Image Upload

Upload your scanned passport photo and signature file inside the designated boxes. Ensure files are properly sized to prevent upload timeouts.

Step 6 — Finalize Application Fees

Click the payment link option, settle your examination processing fees via online gateway channels, and print out your generated confirmation sheet.
