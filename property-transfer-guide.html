Property Transfer Guide

Learn how to transfer property ownership, upload the required documents, pay applicable charges, track your application, and complete the property transfer process through the official State Government or Registration Department portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Government's Registration Department, Revenue Department, or Municipal Corporation portal. These portals provide services related to property transfer, registration, mutation, and ownership records.

Tip: Property registration and property mutation are separate processes. After registering the transfer, you may also need to apply for mutation to update government ownership records.

📝 Step 2 — Submit the Property Transfer Application

Choose the appropriate property transfer service and complete the online application form.

  • Enter buyer and seller details.
  • Provide property information.
  • Enter registration details.
  • Select the reason for transfer (Sale, Gift, Inheritance, Partition, etc.).
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Prepare scanned copies of the required documents before submitting your application.

  • Registered Sale Deed, Gift Deed, or other transfer document.
  • Identity Proof of all parties.
  • Address Proof.
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt.
  • Property Ownership Documents.
  • Other documents required by your State Government.
Ensure that all uploaded documents are clear, complete, and match the information entered in the application form.

💳 Step 4 — Pay Applicable Charges

Complete the online payment for any applicable fees, such as registration charges, mutation fees (if applicable), or other government charges.

  • Pay the required fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use your Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor the progress of your property transfer request.

  • Track verification status.
  • Check document review progress.
  • Respond to additional document requests if required.
  • Monitor approval status.
Keep your application number safe until the property transfer process is successfully completed.

📥 Step 6 — Download Updated Property Records

Once the application is approved, download the updated ownership records or transfer confirmation from the official portal, where available.

  • Verify the updated owner details.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print copies if required for future reference.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Verify all ownership details before submitting the application.
  • Keep copies of all uploaded documents and payment receipts.
  • Apply for mutation if it is required after registration.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Download updated records after approval.
  • Use only the official State Government portal.
Always complete property transfer services through the official State Government or Registration Department portal. Never share your OTP, login credentials, or payment information with unauthorized websites or individuals.
