Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) Website User Guide

Learn how to use the official Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) website for civic services, certificates, property tax, licenses, grievances, and online citizen services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official MCD Website

Open the official MCD website. The homepage provides access to citizen services, announcements, online applications, public notices, and important municipal updates.

Tip: Check the homepage for service alerts, maintenance notices, and new citizen services.

📄 Step 2 — Choose the Service You Need

Select the service that matches your requirement.

  • Birth Certificate
  • Death Certificate
  • Property Tax
  • Trade License
  • Health License
  • Building Plan Approval
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Online Grievance Services

💻 Step 3 — Use Online Citizen Services

Many services can be completed online without visiting an MCD office.

  • Apply for certificates.
  • Pay property tax.
  • Track application status.
  • Download issued certificates.
  • Renew licenses.
Keep your Application Number, Property ID, or Registration Number ready before using online services.

💰 Step 4 — Property Tax Services

Property owners can manage their tax-related services online.

  • Pay Property Tax
  • View Outstanding Dues
  • Download Tax Receipt
  • Update Property Details (where applicable)

📢 Step 5 — Check Notices & Public Information

Stay updated with official MCD announcements, including:

  • Public Notices
  • Tender Notices
  • Recruitment Updates
  • Health Advisories
  • Sanitation Drives
  • Municipal Announcements

☎️ Step 6 — Register Complaints & Grievances

Use the grievance section to report civic issues such as:

  • Garbage Collection
  • Road Damage
  • Streetlight Issues
  • Waterlogging
  • Mosquito Breeding
  • Public Sanitation Problems
After submitting a complaint, save the Complaint Reference Number to track its status.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Use online services whenever available to save time.
  • Keep important IDs and application numbers handy.
  • Download receipts after every successful payment.
  • Track your applications regularly.
  • Read eligibility and document requirements before applying.
  • Verify all information before submitting any online form.
Always use the official MCD website for payments, certificates, and grievance services. Avoid making payments through unofficial websites or unknown links.
