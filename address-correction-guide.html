Address Correction in Municipal Records Guide

Learn how to apply for an address correction in municipal records, upload the required documents, pay applicable fees, track your application, and view the updated records through your Municipal Corporation's official portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation's official Citizen Services or Property Records portal. Most local authorities provide online services for updating address details in municipal records and tracking application status.

Tip: Address correction services may be available for property records, municipal records, tax records, or other local government records, depending on your Municipal Corporation.

📝 Step 2 — Submit the Address Correction Application

Select the Address Correction service and carefully complete the online application form.

  • Enter the Record Number, Property ID, or Application Number (if applicable).
  • Provide the applicant's details.
  • Enter the existing address.
  • Enter the corrected address.
  • Specify the reason for the correction.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Prepare scanned copies of the required documents before applying.

  • Identity Proof.
  • Address Proof.
  • Property Ownership Documents (if applicable).
  • Supporting documents for the requested correction.
  • Other documents required by the Municipal Corporation.
Ensure that all uploaded documents clearly support the address correction request and are valid and legible.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Applicable Fee

If a processing fee is applicable, complete the online payment using the available payment methods.

  • Pay the prescribed correction fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference number for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use your Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor the progress of your correction request.

  • Track verification status.
  • Respond to requests for additional documents.
  • Monitor approval updates.
  • Check application status online.
Keep your Application Number safe until your correction request has been completed.

📥 Step 6 — View the Updated Municipal Record

Once your application is approved, verify that the corrected address has been updated in the municipal records.

  • Review the updated address.
  • Download the updated record if available.
  • Save a digital copy for future reference.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Verify the corrected address before submitting your application.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep your application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Download the updated record after approval if available.
  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation portal.
Always submit address correction requests through the official Municipal Corporation portal. Never share your OTP, login credentials, or payment information with unauthorized websites or individuals.
