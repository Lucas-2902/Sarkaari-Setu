Step 1: Keep Your Credentials Ready

Before opening the link, make sure you have these written down:
- Application Number
- Date of Birth (DOB)

Step 2: Go to the Official NEET Website

Visit the official National Testing Agency (NTA) NEET portal: neet.nta.nic.in

Step 3: Find the Result Link

Once the homepage loads, look for the "Candidate Activity" section or the main notice board.
Click on the active link labeled "NEET UG Scorecard" or "View NEET Result".

Step 4: Login

A new login window will open.
Enter your Application Number and your Date of Birth.
Type in the Security Pin / Captcha exactly as shown on the screen.
Click Submit.

Step 5: Check and Save Your Scorecard

Your digital scorecard will display your subject-wise marks, total score, percentile, All India Rank (AIR), and whether you have qualified.
Click Download or Print to save it as a PDF. You will absolutely need this scorecard during your MCC/State counseling and college admission.

⚠️ Note: If you are trying to check immediately after the NTA announces the scores, the server may crash or slow down significantly due to millions of students trying to log in at once. If the page doesn't load, just give it 15-30 minutes and try again.
