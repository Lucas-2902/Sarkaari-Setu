Passport Seva Portal User Guide

Learn how to use the official Passport Seva Portal to apply for a new passport, renew an existing passport, schedule appointments, track your application, and access passport-related services.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Passport Seva Portal

Open the official Passport Seva Portal. The homepage provides access to passport applications, appointment booking, application tracking, fee details, and citizen services.

Tip: Keep your mobile number and email address active, as important updates are sent through them.

👤 Step 2 — Register or Log In

If you're a new user, create an account using your email ID. Existing users can log in to access passport services.

  • Create a Passport Seva account.
  • Verify your email address.
  • Log in using your registered credentials.
  • Select the Passport Office nearest to your residence.

📝 Step 3 — Apply for a Passport

Choose the appropriate service and complete the online application form carefully.

  • Fresh Passport
  • Passport Renewal / Re-issue
  • Lost or Damaged Passport
  • Change in Personal Particulars
  • Tatkaal Passport (if eligible)
Double-check your personal details before submitting the application to avoid delays.

📄 Step 4 — Upload Documents & Pay the Fee

Depending on your application, you may need to provide supporting documents and pay the applicable passport fee online.

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Date of Birth Proof
  • Additional documents (if applicable)
  • Online Fee Payment

📅 Step 5 — Book Your Appointment

After submitting your application and payment, schedule an appointment at your selected Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK).

  • Select an available date.
  • Choose a convenient time slot.
  • Download or print the appointment receipt.

📦 Step 6 — Track Your Application

Use the "Track Application Status" service to monitor your passport application after your appointment.

  • View application status.
  • Check police verification updates.
  • Track passport dispatch.
  • Confirm delivery status.
Keep your Application Reference Number (ARN) or File Number safe to easily track your application.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply well before your travel date.
  • Carry original documents during your appointment.
  • Arrive at the Passport Seva Kendra on time.
  • Verify all information before submitting your application.
  • Save your Application Reference Number (ARN).
  • Track your application regularly for updates.
Always apply through the official Passport Seva Portal. Never share your login credentials, OTP, or payment information with unauthorized websites or individuals.
