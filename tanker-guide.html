Water Tanker Booking Guide

Learn how to book a water tanker, upload required information, make online payments, track your booking, and receive water supply services through your Water Board, Jal Board, or Municipal Corporation's official portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Water Board, Jal Board, or Municipal Corporation's official website. Most authorities provide online services for booking water tankers, making payments, and tracking booking status.

Tip: Water tanker availability may depend on your location, water demand, and the authority's operating schedule.

📝 Step 2 — Submit Your Booking Request

Select the Water Tanker Booking service and carefully complete the online booking form.

  • Enter your name and contact details.
  • Provide the delivery address.
  • Select the required tanker size (if available).
  • Choose the preferred delivery date and time (if applicable).
  • Review all details before submitting.

📄 Step 3 — Provide Required Information

Depending on your local authority, you may need to provide or upload supporting details.

  • Identity Proof (if required).
  • Address Proof.
  • Consumer/Water Connection Number (if applicable).
  • Previous booking details (if requested).
  • Other information required by the authority.
Ensure your delivery address and contact number are correct to avoid delays in tanker delivery.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Booking Charges

Complete the online payment using the available payment methods.

  • Pay the prescribed tanker booking fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference number for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Booking

Use your Booking Number or Reference Number to monitor the status of your water tanker request.

  • Track booking status.
  • View dispatch updates.
  • Receive delivery notifications.
  • Check estimated delivery information (if available).
Keep your Booking Number safe until the water tanker has been successfully delivered.

🚰 Step 6 — Receive the Water Tanker

On the scheduled delivery date, ensure someone is available at the delivery location to receive the tanker and verify the booking details.

  • Confirm the delivery address.
  • Verify the booking details.
  • Keep the payment receipt for future reference.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Book your tanker in advance during periods of high demand.
  • Provide an accurate delivery address and contact number.
  • Keep your booking reference number and payment receipt safe.
  • Track your booking regularly for updates.
  • Use only the official Water Board, Jal Board, or Municipal Corporation portal.
  • Be available at the delivery location on the scheduled date.
Always book water tankers through the official government portal. Never make payments through unofficial websites or share your OTP, payment details, or login credentials with unauthorized individuals.
