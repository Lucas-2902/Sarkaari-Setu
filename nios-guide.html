NIOS Website User Guide

Learn how to use the official National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) website for admissions, examinations, study materials, assignments, and results.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official NIOS Website

Open the official NIOS website. The homepage provides access to admissions, examinations, student services, public notices, study materials, and learner support.

Tip: During admission and examination periods, check the homepage regularly for the latest notifications and deadlines.

📝 Step 2 — Apply for Admission

If you're a new learner, use the online admission portal to register for Secondary, Senior Secondary, Vocational, or Open Basic Education courses.

  • Create your online account.
  • Fill in your personal and academic details.
  • Upload the required documents.
  • Pay the admission fee.
  • Download the confirmation receipt after submission.

📚 Step 3 — Access Study Materials

After admission, you can download official study materials and learning resources provided by NIOS.

  • Textbooks
  • Self Learning Material (SLM)
  • Tutor Marked Assignment (TMA) Guidelines
  • Practical Instructions
  • Online Learning Resources

🧾 Step 4 — Student Portal Services

Log in to your student dashboard to manage your academic activities.

  • View admission status.
  • Update student details (where permitted).
  • Download Identity Card.
  • Check assignment status.
  • Access examination-related services.
Keep your Enrollment Number and login credentials secure for future use.

📝 Step 5 — Examinations & Results

The NIOS website allows students to access examination-related services.

  • View exam schedules.
  • Download Hall Ticket.
  • Check practical examination notices.
  • View Answer Key (when available).
  • Check Results and download Marksheet information.

📥 Step 6 — Download Important Documents

Always keep copies of important documents for future reference.

  • Admission Confirmation
  • Identity Card
  • Hall Ticket
  • TMA Instructions
  • Result / Marksheet Information
  • Official Circulars

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Complete your admission before the last date.
  • Submit Tutor Marked Assignments (TMAs) on time.
  • Download your Hall Ticket as soon as it is released.
  • Keep your Enrollment Number safe.
  • Read every public notice carefully before taking action.
  • Regularly check the website for examination updates and results.
