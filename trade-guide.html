Trade Licence Application Guide

Learn how to apply for a Trade Licence, renew an existing licence, track your application, make online payments, and download your licence through your Municipal Corporation or local government portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your Municipal Corporation or local government's official Trade Licence portal. The portal provides services for new applications, licence renewal, status tracking, online payments, and certificate downloads.

Tip: Trade Licences are issued by local authorities such as Municipal Corporations or Municipal Councils. The application process and requirements may vary depending on your city or state.

📝 Step 2 — Apply for a New Trade Licence

Select the New Trade Licence service and carefully complete the online application form.

  • Enter applicant details.
  • Provide business information.
  • Enter the business address.
  • Select the type of trade or business activity.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Keep scanned copies of the necessary documents ready before applying.

  • Identity Proof.
  • Address Proof.
  • Business Address Proof.
  • Ownership or Rent Agreement (if applicable).
  • Passport-size Photograph.
  • Other documents required by your local authority.
Ensure all uploaded documents are clear and valid to avoid delays in verification.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Application Fee

Complete the online payment using the available payment methods.

  • Pay the prescribed licence fee.
  • Save the payment receipt.
  • Keep the transaction reference for future use.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

Use the Application Number or Acknowledgement Number to monitor your application's progress.

  • Track verification status.
  • View inspection updates (if applicable).
  • Monitor approval progress.
  • Check application status online.

📥 Step 6 — Download or Renew Your Trade Licence

After approval, download your Trade Licence from the portal. Existing licence holders can also renew their licence online before its expiry date.

  • Download the approved licence.
  • Renew your licence before expiry.
  • Print a copy for display if required by local regulations.
Renew your Trade Licence before its expiry date to avoid penalties or interruptions to your business operations.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Use only the official Municipal Corporation or local government portal.
  • Verify all business details before submitting the application.
  • Keep your application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Upload clear and valid documents.
  • Track your application regularly.
  • Renew your licence on time to maintain compliance.
Never make payments through unofficial websites or share your OTP, login credentials, or banking information with unauthorized individuals. Always use your local authority's official Trade Licence portal.
